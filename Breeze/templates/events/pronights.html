The Local Train

This year, we are thrilled to have the privilege of hosting one of the most widely enjoyed bands in the country. The Local Train are one-of-a-kind and truly embody the spirit of new age rock. Barely a decade old, they’ve already gone on to establish themselves on the radar map of original music in India. They left a million and more captivated by the melodies through their album - Aalas Ke Pedh and their hit singles like Aaftab. With tunes that catch on and music that connects, needless to say that a night with The Local Train is going to be unforgettable - catch them at Breeze 2018.
