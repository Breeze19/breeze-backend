About Breeze

Breeze is the annual techno-cultural & sports fest of Shiv Nadar University with events ranging from a myriad of genres that include music, art, photography, dance, dramatics, quizzing and literary events. The fest is an all-encompassing hub of cultural, technical and sports events that bring together youth from various colleges and universities. Breeze has been a huge success year after year with a cumulative footfall of more than 17,000 people from over 80+ colleges across the country.

We’ve had the pleasure of hosting a plethora of star performers - DJ Anish Sood , Javed Ali and Raghuram in 2014; Neha Kakkar and Band Astitva perform in 2015 and Kanan Gill, Zaeden, Hriday Gattani, Paradigm Shift and DJ Sin in 2016. Breeze has also successfully managed to offer an endless list of real time takeaways in sports, cultural and technical arenas for the participants.

Breeze 2018 is geared to be bigger, better and much grander than any of its previous editions. With ‘A Trip to the 90s’ as our chosen theme, we are bringing three day treat filled with frolic to capture the mood of a weekend getaway to THE decade that was.
